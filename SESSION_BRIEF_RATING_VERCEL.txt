CONTEXT
-------
NETR is a peer-rating basketball app for NYC pickup players.
The iOS app (ios/) is built in SwiftUI. The backend uses Supabase.
The website is deployed on Vercel (separate from the iOS app).

The rating system works as follows:
- Scale: 2.0 – 9.9
- Bayesian prior: 3.2 (average pickup player starting point)
- Regular player ceiling: 9.4
- 9.5–9.9 = "In The League" — locked to verified pros only (NBA/WNBA/G-League)

TIERS (low → high):
  2.0–3.9  Just Lacing Up   (beginner)
  4.0–4.9  Getting Buckets  (casual)
  5.0–5.9  Hold Your Own    (solid pickup)
  6.0–6.9  Gym Rat          (competitive)
  7.0–7.9  Built Different  (top 10%)
  8.0–8.9  Elite            (top 3%)
  9.0–9.4  Certified        (top 1%, highest reachable by pickup)
  9.5–9.9  In The League    (verified pros only — LOCKED)

SKILL CATEGORIES rated per player:
  Shooting, Finishing, Dribbling, Passing, Defense, Rebounding, Basketball IQ

VIBE CATEGORIES (separate score):
  Communication, Unselfishness, Effort, Attitude, Inclusion

SELF-ASSESSMENT (onboarding baseline):
  Players pick from 4-option cards per skill category to seed their initial NETR score.
  Playing level (brand new → semi-pro) also factors into the starting score.
